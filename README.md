# 📚 Study Pilot

**Study Pilot** is an AI-powered study planning application designed to help students organize their syllabus, prioritize topics, and create effective study schedules. By analyzing exam dates and syllabus details, Study Pilot generates personalized timetables that help students study smarter and stay on track.

🌐 **Live Application:**  
https://study-piolet-k32c3657dit23hu6yo7zp2.streamlit.app/

---

## ✨ Features

- 📖 Syllabus management and organization
- 🎯 Smart topic prioritization based on exam dates
- 📅 Personalized timetable generation
- 🤖 AI-assisted study planning
- ⏰ Deadline-aware scheduling
- 📊 Balanced workload distribution
- 💻 Clean and user-friendly interface

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Upload or enter your syllabus.
2. Add exam dates and study details.
3. Study Pilot analyzes:
   - Exam urgency
   - Subject priorities
   - Remaining syllabus
4. Generates a personalized study timetable.
5. Follow the schedule and track your progress.
